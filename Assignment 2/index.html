World Happiness 2023

happiness

noun (n.)

a positive state of emotional well-being that a person experiences

How to we measure happiness? World Happiness Report, published by the Sustainable Development Solution Network, determines the happiness index (score) of each country by analysing the factors that contribute to life satisfaction. The happiness index ranges from 0-10, with 0 representing complete dissatisfaction, and 10 representing complete happiness and satisfaction.

World View of Happiness Index

Gross Domestic Product

GDP per Capita, how much each country produces, divided by the number of people in the country.

Freedom to Make Life Choices

Freedom to choose what to do in life, also includes Human Rights

Healthy Life Expectancy

Physical and mental health which contributes to lifestyle and wellbeing

Social Support

Having someone to count on in times of trouble

Perceptions of Corruption

Corruption in business and government

Generosity

A sense of positive community engagement and human connection

Dystopia

Dystopia is an imaginary country that has the world’s least-happy people. The purpose of establishing Dystopia is to have a benchmark against which all countries can be favorably compared in terms of each of the six key variables.

All definitions are referenced from the 2023 World Happiness Report.
